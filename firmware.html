I'm currently working on an elevator model. I'm using the STM32l746 microcontroller to model an elevator system. The elevator would take an input an elevator floor and move a peripheral motor a desired amount based on the modelled floor plan measurements. Then I will send the data remotely to my phone to tell me the number of people per floor on an elevator. The elevator will also track the people calling the elevator at a specific floor and the floor they subsequently go to, keeping track of real-time people at each floor.
